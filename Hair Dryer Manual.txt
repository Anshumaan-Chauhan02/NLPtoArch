A hairdryer is composed of a user interface.
The user interface consists of a handle and a body.
The handle encloses the power unit. 
The body encloses the propulsion unit and the heating unit.
The power unit consists of the mains cord, the power switch, the junction box, the heating wires, and the motor wires. 
The body consists of a barrel, an intake grill, and a discharge opening. 
The heating unit comprises of a heater core, an insulating sleeve, and a nozzle.
The mains cord imports electricity and transfers it to the power switch.
The power switch transfers electricity to the junction box. 
The heating wires transfer electricity from the junction box to the heater core.
The motor wires transfers electricity from the junction box to the motor.
The propulsion unit consists of a motor and a fan. 
The fan is connected to the motor. 
The intake grill imports air and transfers it to the fan.
The motor transfers mechanical energy to the fan. 
The fan imports air and energizes to flowing air. 
The fan transfers flowing air to the heating unit.
The nozzle consists of a conduit. 
The heater core converts electricity to thermal energy 
The heater core imports air and energizes it to hot flowing air.  
The conduit transfers hot flowing air to the discharge opening. 
The discharge opening exports the hot flowing air.
The handle consists of a hand grip and a power button. 
The power button is connected to the power switch.
